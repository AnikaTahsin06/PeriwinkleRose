Kessa describes as adorableness - a true inspiration, a brand all about passion for crafts and tradition from all across India. From hand-block of deserts of Kutch called Ajrakh to the weaves of Andhra called 'Ikat', not leaving behind silks of Banaras and yes of course the very famous art Sanganer print of Jaipur.
